About the Club

Where Yellow Jackets
build the future.

The AWS Student Builder Group at Georgia Tech is a student led, student driven organization focused on learning the cloud through AWS technologies. We cover security, AI, business analytics, business transformation, and the real world skills employers are actively hiring for.

We give students hands on experience through bootcamps, building sessions, and industry networking, all centered around AWS tools. We formally launch on August 17, 2026, and we're recruiting our founding core team right now.

Core team applications open this summer
Open to all GT majors and class years at launch
Free AWS Skill Builder access and Cloud Quest for all members
