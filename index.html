About the Club

Where Ramblin' Wrecks
build the future.

Founded in Summer 2026 by Gauri Menon, the GT AWS Student Builder Group is a student-led, student-driven organization focused on learning the cloud through AWS technologies. We cover security, AI, business analytics, business transformation, and the real-world skills employers are actively hiring for.

We give students hands-on experience through bootcamps, building sessions, and industry networking — all centered around AWS tools. We formally launch on August 17, 2026, and we're recruiting our founding core team right now.
