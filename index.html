About Us

Who We Are

AWS Student Builder Group at Georgia Tech is a student-led, student-driven organization focused on learning about the cloud through AWS technologies.

Our goal is to teach students about the AWS Cloud and its various use cases — including security, AI, business analytics, and business transformation — and to demonstrate how the cloud accelerates business outcomes.

Beyond theory, AWS SBG at GT gives students hands-on experience through projects built in the AWS Cloud, enabling members to develop both technical and business expertise that is currently in high demand in the industry.

As part of the global AWS Student Builder Groups program, we are bringing the program's mission to life on Georgia Tech's campus: creating a collaborative learning environment and building community within the AWS Builder Center.

Launching August 17, 2026 — be one of our founding members and help shape the future of this community at Georgia Tech.
